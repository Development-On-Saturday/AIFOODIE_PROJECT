About AIFOODIE Team

We met at AIFFEL, an artificial intelligence innovation school. AIFFEL is an educational institution where students learn artificial intelligence and coexist with each other. Our team won first place in the 1st Hackathon hosted by AIFFEL. we have dreamed of becoming a startup by supporting 18 primer batches. Hackathon 2nd and 3rd will conduct food classification under the team name AI FOODIE. We will find and tune the classification model suitable for the food to find the highest recognition rate. In addition, we plan to create a classification model that best suits food.

Experiments & Skills

In order to solve the food image classification problem, we fine-tune the parameters according to our dataset using a model pre-trained with the ImageNet dataset. At this time, the model trained the applications provided by keras with our dataset, evaluated the performance, and selected the optimal model. EifficientNet-B0 showed the highest performance for the dataset without augmentation applied. After that, the performance of the model was improved by applying learning strategies such as applying data augmentation to EifficientNet-B0 and adjusting the learning rate by cosine annealing.
