ABOUT AIFOODIE TEAM

Our team won first place in the 1st Hackathon hosted by AIFFEL. In addition, we have dreamed of becoming a startup by supporting 18 primer batches. Hackathon 2nd and 3rd will conduct food classification under the team name AI FOODIE. We will find and tune the classification model suitable for the food to find the highest recognition rate. In addition, we plan to create a classification model that best suits food.
